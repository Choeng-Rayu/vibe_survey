# Product Overview

## Vibe Survey Platform

**Tagline:** "Turning your Prompt into the Survey form"

Vibe Survey is a Survey-as-Ads marketplace that connects three key stakeholders:

- **Advertisers**: Create and manage surveys to gather market insights
- **Survey Takers**: Complete surveys and earn rewards through mobile wallets
- **Platform Administrators**: Oversee platform operations, content moderation, and user management

## Core Value Proposition

The platform transforms survey creation through AI-powered tools, allowing advertisers to generate high-quality surveys from simple prompts while providing survey takers with a rewarding experience through integrated mobile wallet payouts (ABA Pay, WING, TrueMoney).

## Key Features

- **AI Survey Builder**: Generate surveys from natural language prompts
- **Advanced Targeting**: Sophisticated audience targeting with demographic, geographic, and behavioral criteria
- **Fraud Detection**: Real-time fraud detection with behavioral analysis
- **Mobile Wallet Integration**: Direct payouts to popular mobile wallets
- **Real-time Analytics**: Comprehensive campaign performance and response analytics
- **Multi-format Support**: Import/export surveys in Excel, PDF, and JSON formats

## Business Model

Survey-as-Ads marketplace where advertisers pay for quality responses, survey takers earn rewards for participation, and the platform takes a commission on transactions.
